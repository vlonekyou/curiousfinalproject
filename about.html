About Ayala Land

Ayala Land, Inc. is among the Philippines' largest and most respected property developers. We are committed to building master-planned, sustainable communities that deliver lasting value to residents, investors, and stakeholders.

Beginnings in Makati

Ayala Land’s origins trace back to the transformation of Hacienda Makati — once an undeveloped property — into the vibrant Makati Central Business District, now the Philippines’ premier financial hub.

Leading Listed Property Developer

Established in 1988 and publicly listed in 1991, Ayala Land has since evolved into the country’s leading real estate developer, with a portfolio that spans residential, commercial, and industrial estates.

Mission & Vision

Vision: To enhance land and enrich the lives of more Filipinos.

Mission: We develop sustainable, integrated, and master-planned communities that uplift lives and create long-term value for customers and shareholders.

Legacy of Uplifting Lives

Our legacy goes beyond real estate. We aim to enrich the lives of Filipinos by delivering vibrant environments where families thrive, businesses grow, and communities prosper.
